Have you ever stumbled across a tv show AGES after the masses and had no one to talk to spoiler free?

Are you a couple of episodes behind your favorite show and would love to view people's thoughts episode by episode as you catch up?


TV-tweets is a web scraper dedicated to providing the sense of community that comes with tweeting along with the masses as you watch a show... but days, weeks, months, or even years after the fact!


To get started, search for the show of your choice and select the episode you have just watched to view the top tweets about that particular episode.
